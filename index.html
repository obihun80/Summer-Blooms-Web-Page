Graceful muses gather round...,

Clothed in air and breath of skies...

Gladly joined them choirs of women,
Winged in hope, in flowing arcs...

— Oksana Bihun

Summer Blooms is a vocal ensemble of women winged in hope. We strive for excellence by becoming vessels for the music, bringing it to life with emotional honesty and depth. We perform sacred and secular repertoire that resonates with us most.
